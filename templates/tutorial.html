Chat Tutorial

Welcome to Gesture Chat!

This system helps you communicate using simple hand gestures. Each gesture represents a word that you can use to build sentences and send messages.

Tips for Best Results:

  • Make sure your hand is clearly visible to the camera
  • Use good lighting for better recognition
  • Hold gestures steady for 1-2 seconds
  • Keep your hand at a comfortable distance from the camera
  • Practice gestures slowly and clearly
